The grow room is one system

Pull a lever — watch the cascade. The Grow-Room Levers field guide says failures are almost never one number out of range; they're a mismatch between levers. This lab turns its matrices into something you can poke: stage envelopes, the four balances, root-cause diagnostics, and dew-point / EC-stacking forecasts — the inference layer of the Plant-State dashboard, made playable.
